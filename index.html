Who Am I?

I’m Coach King, the driving force behind Forge Your Fitness. I’ve seen it time and time again—people saying they can’t lose weight, pushing themselves but going in circles. The problem isn’t effort—it’s lack of accountability, discipline, and the right motivation.

Maybe you’ve tried countless programs from other trainers, stuck to strict diets, and still saw no real results. If that sounds familiar, it’s time to change the game. When you train with me, you’re not just getting another plan—you’re getting a coach who keeps you accountable, a strategy that actually works, and the support you need to succeed.

No more wasted effort. No more frustration. Just real progress. Are you ready to forge your fitness and transform for good? Let’s get to work.
